Rivers in Utah

River Rafting Utah - Ogden

The Ogden River, nestled in the picturesque Ogden Valley of northern Utah, offers thrilling river rafting adventures for outdoor enthusiasts of all skill levels. Flowing approximately 35 miles from its headwaters in the Wasatch Mountains to its confluence with the Weber River, the Ogden River boasts exhilarating rapids, stunning scenery, and abundant wildlife. Rafting on the Ogden River provides a unique opportunity to experience the beauty of Utah's wilderness while navigating Class II to Class IV rapids, suitable for both beginners and experienced rafters. The river's diverse terrain, including scenic canyons, forested banks, and open meadows, creates an unforgettable backdrop for your adventure. During your rafting trip, keep an eye out for native wildlife such as mule deer, beavers, and various bird species, adding to the river's natural charm. With professional guides available for guided tours and equipment rentals, you can safely enjoy the excitement of white water rafting on the Ogden River while immersing yourself in the tranquility of nature. Whether you're seeking a family-friendly outing or an adrenaline-fueled adventure, the Ogden River promises an unforgettable rafting experience in the heart of Utah's rugged landscape.

River Rafting Utah - Provo

The Provo River, nestled in the picturesque landscapes of Utah, offers an exhilarating river rafting experience for adventure enthusiasts of all skill levels. With its crystal-clear waters and breathtaking scenery, the Provo River provides a perfect blend of excitement and natural beauty. Rafting down the Provo River allows visitors to immerse themselves in the stunning surroundings of towering cliffs, lush forests, and diverse wildlife. Whether you're seeking a leisurely float or an adrenaline-pumping ride through thrilling rapids, the Provo River caters to all preferences. The river features different sections, each offering unique challenges and opportunities for exploration. Families and beginners can enjoy the gentle currents of the Upper Provo, while experienced rafters can tackle the more challenging rapids of the Lower Provo. Guided rafting tours are available for those seeking expert guidance and safety during their adventure. Additionally, the Provo River offers opportunities for other outdoor activities such as fishing, hiking, and picnicking along its scenic banks. For an unforgettable outdoor experience filled with excitement and natural beauty, plan your river rafting adventure on the Provo River today.
